Infrastructure & Systems Transformation

Victory Christian Centre Trust | Systems Administrator & AV Lead

Network Backbone & TCO Strategy

The Bottleneck: Identified a legacy 100Mbps Fast Ethernet switch on Day One that was throttling the entire office fleet.

I led the transition to a modern UniFi SDN ecosystem. I chose to standardize on UniFi to maintain a "Single Umbrella" management style while ensuring zero ongoing subscription costs, protecting the Trust from long-term OpEx "tax" common in enterprise networking.

  • Performance: Established a 1000Mbps (Gigabit) backbone, increasing throughput by 10x.
  • Physical Infrastructure: Personally repaired and "livened" structured cabling in secondary buildings and terminated a new 24-port patch panel.
  • Management: Deployed a UniFi UDM-SE to centralize firewall, VLAN, and Access Point management.

Cloud VoIP & Logic Engineering

The Failure: Legacy shared extensions prevented the Receptionist from calling or transferring to the Accounts office. Staff were frustrated by cordless phones that failed mid-call due to poor battery life.

I re-architected the communications system to provide professional-grade reliability and mobility.

  • 1:1 Provisioning: Assigned unique extensions to every staff member, eliminating all internal transfer collisions.
  • Auto Attendant (IVR): Designed a self-service menu allowing callers to select their destination, reducing reception workload.
  • Fail-Safe Queues: Implemented a call queue that rings all office phones if no menu option is selected, ensuring zero missed calls.
  • Endpoint Modernization: Replaced all unreliable cordless phones with professional headsets and softphone apps, enabling full laptop-based mobility.
  • ROI: Negotiated a deal for unlimited extensions for a marginal $5/mo increase.

Identity & Data Governance

Migrated fragmented local accounts and disorganized file storage into a secure, unified Microsoft 365 tenant.

  • Entra ID: Centralized identity management with enforced MFA and conditional access.
  • SharePoint Architecture: Built a structured library with Role-Based Access Controls (RBAC) to ensure data was secure and accessible.
