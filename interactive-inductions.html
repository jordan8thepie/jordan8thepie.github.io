Interactive Inductions

Corporate Web Redesign & Platform Modernization

WordPress UI/UX Design Frontend Development Theme Customization

The Objective

The primary goal for this project was to overhaul the existing web presence for Interactive Inductions. The business needed a high-end digital storefront to showcase their specialized multimedia services and software capabilities to potential clients.

My Creative Role

I was responsible for the complete design and creation of the new website. This involved moving the brand into a modern WordPress environment, selecting a professional theme foundation, and heavily customizing the frontend to create a seamless, "showcase-first" user experience.

Key Contributions

UI/UX Overhaul

Architected a new navigation structure that allowed users to easily explore the business's portfolio and service offerings.

WordPress Deployment

Managed the full setup, including hosting configuration, security hardening, and plugin integration.

Responsive Design

Ensured the "showcase" look remained consistent across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

Asset Management

Optimized high-resolution images and marketing materials to ensure fast load times without sacrificing quality.

Project Summary

Type: Business Showcase

Stack: WordPress, PHP, CSS3

Focus: Brand Modernization

"My work focused on elevating the brand's digital identity, allowing the technical complexity of their services to be presented in a professional, accessible format."
