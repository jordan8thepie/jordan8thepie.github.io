Network Backbone & TCO Strategy

The Bottleneck: Identified a legacy 100Mbps Fast Ethernet switch on Day One that was throttling the entire office campus.

I standardized the campus on a modern UniFi ecosystem. I chose to stick with UniFi specifically because it allowed for a "Single Umbrella" management style with zero ongoing subscription costs, protecting the Trust from the annual "hardware tax" common in enterprise networking.

  • Established a 1000Mbps Gigabit backbone, increasing throughput by 10x.
  • Decommissioned fragmented legacy gear in favor of a UniFi UDM-SE and PoE Switching.
  • Restored structured cabling in secondary buildings, terminating into a new 24-port patch panel.
Operational Wins
  • 10x Network Speed Increase
  • $0 Annual Hardware Subscriptions
  • Restored Reception-to-Accounts Link
  • 100% Mobile Workforce Ready

Cloud VoIP & Communication Logic

The Failure: Circular transfer loops meant Reception could not call the Accounts office due to "Shared Extension" collisions.
1:1 Extension Provisioning

Migrated the organization to a Cloud PBX with unlimited extensions for just $5/mo extra. Every staff member was given a unique digital identity, finally allowing seamless internal transfers and direct-dialing.

The Frustration: Legacy cordless phones were causing dropped calls due to failing batteries and poor range.
Softphone & Headset Migration

Eliminated physical technical debt by decommissioning all cordless phones. Transitioned the office to USB/Bluetooth headsets and softphone apps, ensuring reliability and allowing staff to take calls from any laptop.

Automation: Auto Attendant & Fail-Safe Queues

To reduce the administrative burden on reception, I engineered a logic-driven Auto Attendant (IVR). Callers can now select their destination (e.g., "Press 1 for Accounts"). I implemented a Fail-Safe Queue so if no option is selected, the system "blasts" all office phones, ensuring no call is ever abandoned.

Identity & Data Sovereignty

Entra ID & MFA

Replaced fragmented local accounts with centralized Microsoft Entra ID. Enabled Single Sign-On (SSO) and enforced MFA, securing the organization's digital perimeter.

SharePoint Governance

Moved the "Server" from a personal OneDrive to SharePoint Online. Implemented Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to ensure departmental data remained secure yet accessible.

Endpoint Modernization & Autopilot

Transition to Zero-Touch Deployment
The Legacy Process: No standardized "Magic Image" or deployment plan existed. Every machine was manually configured, with software and security updates installed one-by-one—a process taking hours per device.

I modernized the asset lifecycle by implementing Microsoft Intune and Windows Autopilot. This allowed the organization to move from "Manual Setup" to a cloud-based provisioning model.

Automated Software Delivery

Configured Intune to push essential apps (M365, ProPresenter, Security Tools) automatically upon first login, ensuring every staff member had the exact same "Ready-to-Work" experience.

Value-Driven Procurement

Strategically sourced high-spec refurbished hardware, using Autopilot to "re-provision" them to enterprise standards, saving the Trust thousands in capital expenditure.

Modernization Impact
  • 90% Reduction in setup time per device.
  • Enforced Compliance: BitLocker and MFA are now mandatory, not optional.
  • Cloud-Ready: Machines can now be shipped directly to a remote staff member's home and be fully configured over Wi-Fi.

Managed Communications & Exchange Governance

Enterprise Mail & Data Sovereignty

I led the migration to Exchange Online, transforming a fragmented email setup into a governed enterprise environment.

  • Global Address List (GAL) Standardization: Audited and standardized all internal email addresses and display names, creating a professional, searchable company directory.
  • Shared Mailbox Optimization: Converted departmental accounts (e.g., accounts@, office@ info@) into Shared Mailboxes. This enabled collaborative access for the team while reducing licensing costs and improving security.
  • Automated Compliance: Implemented a Global Transport Rule to append a standardized legal disclaimer to all outgoing domain emails, ensuring professional branding and regulatory compliance.
