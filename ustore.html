U-store Self Service

Web Design & Digital Identity for Storage Solutions

Web Design Frontend Development Information Architecture

Project Overview

U-store Self Service required a modern, clean, and trustworthy web presence to showcase their storage facilities. The goal was to take complex information—pricing tiers, unit sizes, and security features—and present them in a way that was easy for potential customers to digest.

My Creative Approach

I designed the site from the ground up, focusing on a "User-First" philosophy. I translated the business's physical services into a logical digital structure, ensuring that the most important information (location, unit availability, and contact details) was always front and center.

Design & Implementation

Visual Hierarchy

Created a clean aesthetic that builds user trust through professional typography and strategic use of whitespace.

Service Presentation

Designed custom pricing and unit tables to help users quickly identify the storage solution that fits their needs.

Performance Optimization

Ensured fast load times and responsive behavior, as many users search for storage solutions while on the move via mobile devices.

Content Strategy

Organized the business's key value propositions (Security, Access, and Price) into a high-converting homepage layout.

Project Details

Project Type: Business Showcase

Tools Used: HTML5, CSS3, Responsive Design

Focus: Customer Conversion

"For Ustore, the challenge was to make storage feel accessible and simple. The final design focuses on clarity and ease of use."
