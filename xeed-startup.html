The Digital Challenge

In 2016, I was tasked with building the digital presence for XEED, a startup designed to be a multi-service umbrella. it was an Idea that my old boss at Grand Training had. My objective was to create a cohesive platform that could house wildly diverse ventures—from property services to technical repairs—under one professional brand.

Web Architecture & Design Role

My work on this project was focused entirely on the digital execution. I acted as the sole designer and developer to bring the business concept to life on the web:

  • Complex Navigation Design: Created a UI that allowed users to easily toggle between service categories like Computer Repairs, Painting, and Building without losing brand continuity.
  • Brand Integration: Built the landing pages and intake funnels for various sub-brands, ensuring they all adhered to the parent company's aesthetic.
  • Public Credit: The site was successfully deployed and maintained, featuring my design credit in the site's footer throughout its operation.
