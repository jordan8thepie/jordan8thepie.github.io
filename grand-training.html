The 2024 Modernization Project

For Grand Training, I was tasked with a complete frontend overhaul and backend optimization. By migrating the legacy design to Bootstrap 5, I brought the platform up to 2024 standards—improving mobile responsiveness, accessibility, and user engagement.

Architectural Scaling

I utilized a modified version of the Computer Learning Centre codebase, adapting the core CodeIgniter 3 framework to meet Grand Training's specific brand identity and course structure. This involved:

  • UI Modernization: Implementing a clean, grid-based layout using the latest Bootstrap 5 components.
  • Backend Refinement: Customizing the CI3 controllers and models to handle unique Grand Training curriculum data.
  • Internal IT Support: Maintaining the hardware and network infrastructure to ensure zero downtime for active classes.

Full-Spectrum Technical Support

Beyond development, I provided on-site IT support, troubleshooting hardware issues, managing network configurations, and ensuring that the training environment remained stable for both students and instructors.
